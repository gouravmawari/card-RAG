 # CBSE Class 10 Science Exam Pattern Context

  ## Most Tested Topics (High Frequency)
  - Chemical Reactions and Equations (Focus on balancing)
  - Life Processes (Focus on digestion and circulation)
  - Electricity (Focus on Ohm's Law and circuit diagrams)
  - Light (Focus on Mirror/Lens formulas)

  ## Common Question Types
  - **Define:** Provide exact scientific definitions.
  - **Compare/Contrast:** Distinguish between two concepts (e.g., Aerobic vs Anaerobic).
  - **Calculate:** Numerical problems involving formulas (e.g., Resistance, Power, Focal length).
  - **Explain/Describe:** Step-by-step processes (e.g., How a plant absorbs water).
  - **Identify/Spot the Error:** Given a wrong equation or circuit, identify what is incorrect.

  ## Tricky Phrasing Patterns
  - "State the law of..."
  - "Differentiate between..."
  - "What happens when [X] is added to [Y]..."
  - "An object is placed at [distance] from a mirror..."

  ## Real Past Exam Question Examples (Style Reference)
  1. "Define an acid. How does it react with a metal carbonate?"
  2. "Calculate the equivalent resistance of three resistors connected in parallel."
  3. "Differentiate between artery and vein."
